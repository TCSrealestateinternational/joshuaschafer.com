About Joshua

A Life Built
With Intention.

Joshua Schafer didn't stumble into the life he's living — he built it, one deliberate decision at a time. As an educator, his passion has always been simple: help people understand things more clearly and live more confidently as a result.

As a husband and homeschool father, that passion came home. Choosing to educate his own children wasn't just a parenting decision — it was a declaration about what matters most. The family dinner table, the long afternoon lessons, the awkward questions, the patient answers — that's where the real education happens.

Joshua is also an entrepreneur who believes financial literacy and investing are life skills every family deserves access to. He speaks openly about wealth-building principles, the mindset shifts required for long-term success, and why so many people overcomplicate what is fundamentally simple.

At the core of everything Joshua does is a bedrock of faith — a conviction that purpose-driven work, grounded in integrity, is how you leave something behind worth leaving. That message resonates whether he's in a conference room, on a podcast, or at his kitchen table.
